Cell Reports
DOI: 10.placeholder-20250504000228
Li et al. discovered that the ER-PM tether protein Syt1 regulates MAMP-induced plasmodesmata (PD) closure by sensing PI(4,5)P2 levels and interacting with ANN4 to activate PD-associated calcium signaling, leading to callose deposition at PDs. This finding reveals a novel mechanism by which plants limit cell-to-cell connectivity during innate immune responses, enhancing our understanding of how intercellular communication is modulated to restrict pathogen spread.
